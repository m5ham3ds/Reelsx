Mission

Our mission is to make it easy for everyone to create beautiful Quran videos for educational and dawah purposes. We believe in spreading the beauty of the Quran through modern technology.

API Credits

Quran Data API

We use the Alquran.cloud API for Quranic text and translations. This API provides accurate Uthmani script and various translation options.

Audio Recitations

Audio recitations are provided by the EveryAyah.com API, offering high-quality recitations from various renowned Qurra.

Font Libraries

We utilize Google Fonts and Font Awesome for beautiful typography and icons.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this service really free?

Yes! This service is completely free to use.

Can I use the generated images and videos commercially?

Yes, you can use the generated content for personal, educational, or commercial purposes. We only ask that you don't claim the tool or technology as your own.

Which reciters are available?

We offer a variety of renowned reciters including Sheikh Abdul Basit, Sheikh Al-Minshawi, Sheikh Al-Husary, Sheikh Sudais, and many others.

Can I contribute to this project?

Absolutely! We welcome contributions from developers, translators, and Quran enthusiasts. Please contact us for more information.

Is there an API available?

Currently, we don't offer a public API, but we're considering it for future updates based on community interest.

How to Use Quran Video Maker

Step-by-Step Guide

1

Select Your Surah

Choose a Surah from the dropdown menu. All 114 Surahs of the Quran are available.

2

Choose Ayah Range

Select the starting and ending Ayahs to generate multiple images at once.

3

Select Font & Reciter

Choose your preferred Arabic font and Quran reciter for audio.

4

Customize Appearance (Optional)

Adjust colors, font size, background, and other design elements in the Advanced Settings.

5

Generate Images & Audio

Click the "Generate" button to create your Ayah images and download audio files.

6

Download Your Content

Download individual images/audio or get everything as a zip file.

Tips & Tricks

  • Custom Backgrounds: Upload your own image to use as a background.
  • Aspect Ratios: Choose different aspect ratios based on where you'll share your content (YouTube, Instagram, TikTok).
  • Translations: Add translations in various languages to make your content more accessible.
  • SRT Files: Download SRT subtitle files for video editing software compatibility.
  • Complete Surah: Download the complete audio for any surah with the "Complete Surah" button.
  • Preview: Use the real-time preview to see how your changes affect the design before generating.
